About SMTECHHUB

A decade of delivering enterprise-grade IT infrastructure, cybersecurity, and managed technology solutions across India.

Our Story

From a Vision to Enterprise IT Partner

SMTECHHUB – Smart Multi-Technology Hub was founded in 2015 by a team of passionate IT professionals who saw a gap in the market: businesses needed a single, reliable technology partner who could handle everything from network design to cybersecurity and cloud migrations — without the complexity of managing multiple vendors.

Starting with a small team of 8 engineers in Noida, we quickly built a reputation for delivering on time, on budget, and to specification. Our first major project — a 500-node network rollout for a leading NBFC — put us on the map. Word spread, and within two years we had expanded to Mumbai and over 50 enterprise clients.

Today, with 50+ certified engineers, office in Andheri East, Mumbai, and partnerships with the world's leading technology vendors, SMTECHHUB stands as one of India's most trusted IT infrastructure and cybersecurity solution providers.

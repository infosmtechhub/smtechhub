About SMTECHHUB

SMTECHHUB – Smart Multi-Technology Hub is a Mumbai-based IT Infrastructure, Cybersecurity, and Managed Technology Solutions company serving businesses across India.

Our Story

Your Trusted Technology Partner

SMTECHHUB – Smart Multi-Technology Hub is a Mumbai-based IT Infrastructure, Cybersecurity, and Managed Technology Solutions company. We serve businesses of all sizes — from startups and SMEs to large enterprises — providing end-to-end technology services under one roof.

Our team of certified engineers and technology consultants brings deep expertise across networking, server infrastructure, cloud platforms, and cybersecurity. We deliver solutions that are secure, scalable, and aligned with your business goals — eliminating the complexity of managing multiple IT vendors.

Headquartered in Andheri East, Mumbai, SMTECHHUB operates across India, supporting clients in BFSI, healthcare, manufacturing, education, government, and retail sectors with reliable technology that keeps their businesses running at full speed.

IT Infrastructure

Servers, networking, structured cabling, data centers, and wireless solutions.

Cybersecurity

Firewalls, VAPT, endpoint security, ISO 27001, and SOC services.

Managed IT

AMC, NOC monitoring, help desk, onsite support, and IT outsourcing.

Cloud Solutions

Google Workspace, Microsoft 365, cloud migration, and backup & DR.
